Smart Irrigation system using IOT

A smart irrigation system leverages IoT technology to automate and optimize water usage in agriculture. It involves sensors to monitor soil moisture, temperature, and humidity, and actuators to control water flow. By collecting real-time data, the system intelligently schedules irrigation, reducing water wastage and ensuring optimal plant growth. This technology not only conserves water resources but also improves crop yields and reduces labor costs.
